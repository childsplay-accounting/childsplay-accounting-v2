# Product Definition: Childsplay Accounting

## Purpose
A modular online management, administrative and bookkeeping system, starting off with a Client Information Database.

## Core Rules

1. **Absolute data integrity.** No duplication. Every piece of data must have a single source of truth.
2. **Deeply downscaled Minimum Viable Product.** Focus exclusively on:
   - Active client profiles
   - Registers
   - Automated email/communication hooks
   - No legacy data imports yet
